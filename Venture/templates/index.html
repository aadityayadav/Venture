Who are we?


Venture is a platform that allows you to find the best suitable job for you within your locality. We firmly believe that nobody understands the market and demands of particular locality the way a local person does. By providing such a platform, we help increasing the employment while helping the job-seeking population reach their maximum potential. This space is also perfect for employers, who require particular skill-sets from their employees. This mostly helps our small and local businesses, that usually find their sales to restrained to their own localities. By finding a resident of the same locality, they will be able to find an employee with unmatched market knowledge. It is a platform helpful to everyone
